About me

I'm an undergrad studying ecology1 at McGill University in Montreal, Canada. I am broadly interested in how we can operate responsibly within our broader environmental context. In particular, I'm quantitative tools and approaches that can help us understand, conserve, and restore ecosystems.

I currently work as a research assistant in Dr. Brian Leung's lab, where I help build computational models of large-scale ecological phenomena to support decision making processes. Most recently, we have been developing an approach to better understand recent trends in global rates of biological invasions. This project builds upon work I did this past summer, which was generously funded by a Bieler Undergraduate Research Award. We are currently drafting a manuscript with collaboraters.i
